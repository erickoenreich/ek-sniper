Setup guide

This app requires four environment variables set in Netlify → Site settings → Environment variables:

EBAY_APP_ID Your eBay developer App ID (Client ID)
EBAY_CERT_ID Your eBay developer Cert ID (Client Secret)
EBAY_DEV_ID Your eBay developer Dev ID
EBAY_USER_TOKEN Your eBay user auth token (from developer portal → get token)

Get your credentials at developer.ebay.com. The EBAY_USER_TOKEN is your personal eBay account token — required to place bids on your behalf.
